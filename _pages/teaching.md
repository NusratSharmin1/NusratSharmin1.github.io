## Instructor of Record

**ECON 1123: Principles of Economics — Microeconomics** *(Winter 2026, forthcoming)*
<br>
*Department of Economics, University of Oklahoma*

This is an intensive one-month online course introducing students to the core 
principles of microeconomics. Topics covered include supply and demand, market 
equilibrium, elasticity, efficiency, market failures, consumer choice, production 
theory, and market structures including perfect competition, monopoly, monopolistic 
competition, and oligopoly. The course concludes with an introduction to game theory.
